Tabentes Luas

Cleanse me of all.

Rot flows through you as me, me as you. Life is lost by those who fail to find what corrupts them. But are innocence and purity even real in a life like this? Birth is the beautiful Rot that force everything to rise from nothing. I suppose that makes death the ugly cleansing, the true return to abyss and escape from meaning.

If I could just touch it without crumbling, collapsing, and corrugating myself.

Every part of us is too weak to that which transcends what we know too well. What do I do then? All I know how to do is Love, apologize, and forgive. Are those enough to save me from the Rot of being, or must I desecrate the self I've built, crucify the ego, and subject myself to diminishment beyond comprehension?

An answer would be to tear apart what it means to be a man - I am just a man.

We're all captives in this monumental little world that we oh so wish we could control. But we are driven instead, pushed to the limits of what our self-gratifying minds can handle. There is no solution but the silent rage of unknowning if our loving tools make us "good enough" - for who?

All there is is that which our Rots touch, that which our web of misunderstood connections and faded feelings are comprised of. Spin me, take me, crush me. To feel small is to really connect. Atomize the body to free the dormant soul, the true bearer of the corruption.

If you know Love as I believe to then let it be.

No man is free from the ultimate release, the one I dodge by name as I hope to its counterpart. Don't wait for it, but don't force it. Love escapes time, the bringer of all things. Let it be the one to drive you and build everything in your web, for otherwise we make ourselves larger than we or the world can handle.

Love frees, regulates, and cleanses us in the absence or meantime as nothing beyond approaches silently and without heed.
